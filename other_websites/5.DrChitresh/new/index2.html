Hi, I am Dr. Chitresh Kumar Sharma (M.B.B.S., M.S., M.Ch., F.MAS., F.A.C.S.) currently working as Associate Professor Surgical Oncology at AIIMS Bilaspur, Himachal Pradesh. I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers in the past. I did my M.B.B.S. and M.S. at Institute of Medical Sciences, Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi. I have completed my M.ch. from Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences, Lucknow.

For more details, check my CV or hit me up on my email.
